Appointments

We recommend booking appointments in advance to ensure availability. Walk-ins are welcome but subject to availability.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer gift certificates?

Yes, we offer gift certificates for all our services and packages. They can be purchased in-person at our salon or by phone.

What forms of payment do you accept?

We accept cash, all major credit cards, and mobile payment options like Apple Pay and Google Pay.

Do you offer services for children?

Yes, we provide haircuts and styling services for children of all ages. We strive to make the experience comfortable and enjoyable for our youngest clients.

What if I'm not satisfied with my service?

Your satisfaction is our priority. If you're not completely happy with your service, please let us know within 7 days, and we'll do our best to make it right.
